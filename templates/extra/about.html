Welcome!

I am a bioinformatician currently working at the Florimond Desprez Group. I bring my expertise to the Research Unit, enabling crop breeders to make faster and more informed decisions.

About me

I develop computational solutions for agronomy applications, for instance identifying genetic markers in plants or predicting their resistance profiles using technologies such as next-generation sequencing, DNA chips, mass spectrometry and omics data. Before working in industry, I contributed to research projects in fisheries and conservation biology at the CNRS. I have been fortunate to collaborate on several publications.

Why this blog?
