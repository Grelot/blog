Welcome!

I am a bioinformatician research scientist based in France currently working at the Florimond Desprez Group.

I build software and data workflows to help breeders make better decisions faster.

What I do

I design tools to analyze omics data. That means turning complex biological information into actionable insights with automation, reproducibility, and scalability in mind.

I care a lot about making data workflows that don't just work, but last.

Before that

Over the last decade, I contributed to population genomics studies for fisheries and marine biology at the CNRS.

I have been fortunate to collaborate on a number of scientific publications.

Why this blog?

This blog is where I stash ideas, findings, and methods picked up along the way. Happy reading!
