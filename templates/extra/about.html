Pierre-Edouard Guerin is a Bioinformatician research scientist. He works at the Research Unit of Florimond Desprez Group. His expertise lies in data engineering, genetics and biotechnology.

He develops innovative software and workflows to analyse omics data for industrial crop breeding. His work supports breeders in making faster and more informed selections, leading to enhanced plants and higher yields for farmers.

Prior to joining Florimond Desprez Group, Pierre-Edouard was a bioinformatician at the CNRS. During 5 years, under the direction of Dr. Stephanie MANEL, he contributed to numerous scientific articles. He collaborated in many European projects focusing on fish stock assessment and management. He received his master's degree in Bioinformatics from the University of Paris.
