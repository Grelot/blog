Welcome!

I am a bioinformatician research scientist based in France currently working at the Florimond Desprez Group. I contribute to the Research Unit, supporting crop breeders in making faster and more informed decisions.

About me

I design tools to analyze omics data. That means turning complex biological information into actionable insights with automation, reproducibility, and scalability in mind. I care a lot about making data workflows that do not just work, but last. My background bridges computer science and life sciences, and I have a broad interest in applying computational approaches to biological questions. Before working in industry, I contributed to population genomics studies for fisheries and marine biology at the CNRS. I have been fortunate to collaborate on a number of scientific publications.
