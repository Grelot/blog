I am Pierre-Edouard Guerin, a Bioinformatician research scientist. I currently work at the Research Unit of Florimond Desprez Group. My expertise lies in data engineering, genetics and biotechnology.

I develop innovative software and workflows to analyse omics data for industrial crop breeding. My work supports breeders in making faster and more informed selections, leading to enhanced plants and higher yields for farmers.

Prior to joining Florimond Desprez Group, I was a bioinformatician at the CNRS. I contributed to numerous scientific publications for many European projects focusing on fish stock assessment and management.
