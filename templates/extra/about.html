Welcome!

I am a bioinformatician currently working at the Florimond Desprez Group. I bring my expertize to the Research Unit, supporting crop breeders in making faster and more informed decisions.

About me

My work is devoted to developing computational solutions for agronomy applications, such as identifying genetic markers in plants or predicting their resistance profiles from various technologies (e.g. next-generation sequencing, DNA chips, mass spectrometry and omics data). I program worflows that turn complex biological information into actionable insights with automation, reproducibility, and scalability in mind. Before working in industry, I contributed to research projects for fisheries and marine biology at the CNRS. I have been fortunate to collaborate on several publications.
